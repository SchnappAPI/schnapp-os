## Goal
The owner's original objective, in their terms.

## Facts established
Constraints, values, preferences settled this session that are not re-derivable from the repo.

## Decisions + reasoning
What was chosen and **why** - the reasoning, not just the choice. Link the ADR if one was written.

## Actions + outcomes
What was done and what resulted; what worked, what failed and why (so it is not re-walked).

## Status + next steps
Where it stands now; what the next session does first.

## Open questions / edge cases
Unresolved forks, owner-only follow-ups (with exact commands), known risks.

## Copy-paste primer (new session)
Two or three sentences a fresh session can paste to resume: the state, the resume point, the next
action. Exclude the exploratory back-and-forth - only what carries the work forward.
